SOS Group, Inc. · Boston, MA · Associate Product Manager

2021 – 2023

Product & UX at SOS Group

The Problem

The team needed better visibility into customer behavior — what was blocking conversions, and where the product experience fell short. Internally, an essential vend-testing workflow took two weeks and was a constant bottleneck.

The Approach

Working across product, operations, and marketing, I ran end-to-end UX research to surface friction and bring the customer’s voice into decisions — then redesigned the vend-testing workflow from the ground up to remove the operational bottleneck.

The Impact

Vend-testing dropped from two weeks to ten minutes, 40+ customer interviews fed directly into the roadmap, and research-driven changes improved conversion — with new SOPs adopted across the operations team.

Results
  • 2 weeks → 10 minutes vend-testing workflow — a major efficiency gain
  • 40+ customer interviews feeding directly into the product roadmap
  • Improved conversion rates through research-driven product changes
  • SOPs authored and adopted across the operations team
  • 3 new operations team members trained
  • Customer insights presented to leadership shaping product & marketing
My Role & Responsibilities
  • Designed a vend-testing workflow that cut testing time from 2 weeks to 10 minutes
  • Led end-to-end UX research: built frameworks, ran 40+ interviews, synthesised findings
  • Presented customer insights to leadership to inform product and marketing
  • Authored SOPs and trained 3 new operations team members
  • Collaborated across product, operations, and marketing on bottleneck analysis
The key win

The biggest win wasn’t a feature — it was rethinking an internal process everyone had accepted. Questioning the two-week vend-testing workflow turned a constant bottleneck into a ten-minute task.
