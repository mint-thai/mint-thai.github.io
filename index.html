CV

Hi, I’m Mint. I help build data-driven solutions that people actually use.

My work sits at the intersection of product, data, and AI. I've worked across startups, finance, retail, higher education, and applied AI, but regardless of the industry, I've always been drawn to the same kind of work: understanding complex problems, defining what needs to be built, and working with teams to bring solutions to life.

I’m currently leading AI initiatives at Thompson Rivers University, including an AI-powered salmon monitoring system developed in partnership with Indigenous communities and a GenAI platform that helps educators transform and adapt teaching materials for different audiences.

Before TRU, I led UX research and product initiatives at an early-stage retail tech startup in Boston. Earlier in my career, I worked in customer service, banking, and corporate finance. Those experiences taught me how to understand different perspectives, navigate competing priorities, and communicate effectively across teams.

Today, I bring those experiences together to connect business needs, user insights, and technical execution — building products, systems, and workflows that people actually want, need, and use.
