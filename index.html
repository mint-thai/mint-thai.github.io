Hi, I’m Mint. I help turn early-stage ideas into real solutions — for the people they’re built for.

I’m a project and product manager with a background spanning business, finance, startups, data science, and applied AI. My biggest strength isn’t confined to business, product, data, or engineering — it’s connecting them. It’s knowing how to move between them to get things shipped.

I’m leading AI initiatives at Thompson Rivers University, including an AI-powered salmon monitoring system built in partnership with Indigenous communities and a GenAI platform that helps educators transform and adapt learning materials for different audiences.

Before TRU, I led UX research and product initiatives at an early-stage retail tech startup in Boston. Earlier in my career, I worked in customer service, banking, and corporate finance. Those experiences taught me how to work with diverse people, understand competing perspectives, and communicate ideas in ways that resonate with different audiences. Today, those skills help me connect technical teams, stakeholders, and users to build solutions that people actually want and need.
